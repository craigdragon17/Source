Dr. Andrews releases 'Throw Like a Pro' app to help reduce elbow injuries

Dr. James Andrews and Dr. Kevin Wilk have released the "Throw Like a Pro" app, designed to help reduce the number of elbow injuries, speciffically Tommy John surgery. They "anticipate a 60% reduction is overuse related baseball injuries in the coming years" if app is used properly, according to the press release.
